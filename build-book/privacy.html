Build Book Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 July 2026

MK VISION (“we”, “our” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled in Build Book.

Information you provide

Build Book may store project details, diary notes, tasks, estimates, material records, income entries and expense entries.

Location

Location permission may be used to show local weather after you grant permission. Build Book does not require continuous background location tracking.

Advertising

Build Book uses Google AdMob. Google and its partners may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interactions and diagnostic information to provide and measure advertising.

Third-party services

The app may use Google Play Services, Google AdMob, Expo and weather-data providers.

Use of information

  • Provide app features
  • Save user records
  • Show local weather
  • Diagnose errors and improve performance
  • Display and measure ads

Data retention and deletion

Locally stored records remain until deleted, app storage is cleared or the app is uninstalled.

Children

Build Book is not directed to children under 13.
