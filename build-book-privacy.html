Build Book Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2026

MK VISION ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Build Book mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and the choices available to users. By using Build Book, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information You Provide

Build Book helps users manage construction projects and related records. Depending on how you use the app, you may voluntarily create and store information such as:

  • Project names
  • Client information
  • Site addresses
  • Daily diary notes
  • Tasks and reminders
  • Income and expense records
  • Material and labour records
  • Bills and receipts
  • Project photographs
  • Other information entered by you

2. Device Permissions

Build Book may request the following permissions depending on the features you use.

Camera

Used only to capture project photos, bills, receipts, and construction progress images.

Photos / Media

Allows you to select existing images from your device and attach them to project records.

Storage

Used only for saving and accessing project-related files or images that you choose.

Notifications

Used to send reminders for tasks, projects, or important application notifications. You may disable notifications from your device settings at any time.

Location (Optional)

If location-based features are enabled, the app may request your location only to improve those features. Location access is optional and can be disabled through your device settings.
